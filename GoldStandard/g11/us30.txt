User Story: 
As an NSF employee, I want to have editorial-planning recommendations, so that I can understand how they might impact an eventual CMS / future web presence.

Acceptance Criteria:
1. The editorial-planning recommendations should be accessible to NSF employees.
2. The recommendations should present ta gap analysis to identify discrepancies between the current CMS capabilities and the future web presence goals.
3. The roadmap of the recommendations should be presented, outlining the prioritized actions and estimated timelines.
4. The recommendations could be modified. And the history should be labelled for review. 
5. The recommendations could be collaborated by other employeed, such as rating or commenting.
6. The updates of the recommendations should be notified to all NSF employees.
7. The recommendations could be serched and filtered via keywords.
8. The recommendations could be prioritized in the working plan.
