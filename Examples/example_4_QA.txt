Acceptance Criteria:
<start>
1. The website should have a visible and easily accessible feedback submission feature on all relevant pages.
2. The feedback form should have the following mandatory fields: Name, Email Address, and Feedback Message.
3. Upon successful submission of feedback, the website should display a confirmation message to the user.
4. The website should send an email notification to the website owners containing the submitted feedback details, including the user's name, email address, and feedback message.
5. The feedback submission feature should be accessible and fully functional on different devices and screen sizes, including desktop, mobile, and tablet.
6. The feedback submission feature should comply with applicable privacy laws and regulations, ensuring the protection of user information and maintaining user anonymity if desired.
7. The Name field in the feedback form should only accept alphabetical characters and should have a minimum length of 2 characters and a maximum length of 50 characters.
8. The Email Address field in the feedback form should validate the input to ensure it is in a valid email format.
9. The Feedback Message field in the feedback form should have a minimum length of 10 characters and a maximum length of 500 characters.
<end>
