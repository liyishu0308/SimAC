Acceptance Criteria:
<start>
1. Swiping/tapping a notification takes the user directly to the message.
2. View shows conversation — if the new message was a reply, then it’s displayed above the original.
3. The message count is updated.
4. A message is marked read after it’s displayed.
5. The application should provide appropriate settings to customize the behavior and appearance of notifications.
6. Perform stress testing by sending multiple notifications to the device and confirming that they are all received and displayed without any delays or performance issues.
7. The push notification service/API is integrated from a third-party platform. 
<end>
