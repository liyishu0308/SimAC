User Story: 
As a user, I want to view thumbnail sequences of video segments.	

Acceptance Criteria (generated from Model #1)
1. The user should be able to access the thumbnail sequences of video segments from the video player interface.
2. The thumbnail sequences should be displayed in a visually appealing and organized manner, allowing the user to easily navigate and identify different segments.
3. Each thumbnail should represent a specific video segment and provide enough information for the user to understand the content of that segment.
4. The thumbnail sequences should include a scrollbar or pagination feature to accommodate a large number of video segments.
5. The user should be able to click or tap on a thumbnail to start playing the corresponding video segment from that point.
6. The video player should provide options to zoom in or enlarge the thumbnail sequences for better visibility on smaller screens.
